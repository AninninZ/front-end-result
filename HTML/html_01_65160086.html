Equations1 : (H2 + Cl2 => HCl)
Hydrochloric acid (HCl), also known as muriatic acid

   It is a colorless solution with a distinctive pungent smell. It is classified as a strong acid.

Equations2 : (Na + HCl => NaCl + H2)
Sodium chlorid (NaCl), commonly known as salt

   Sodium chloride is the salt most responsible for the salinity of seawater and of the extracellular fluid of many multicellular organisms.
